# Hospital Management System - Executive Summary

## Overview

A modern, cloud-based Hospital Management System designed to digitize healthcare operations, improve patient care, and drive business growth for individual doctors, group practices, and hospitals.

---

## The Problem

Healthcare providers face these challenges:
- **Manual Processes**: Paper-based records, phone calls for appointments
- **Poor Online Presence**: Difficult for patients to find and book appointments
- **Inefficient Operations**: Double bookings, missed follow-ups, scattered data
- **Limited Patient Engagement**: No systematic way to communicate and gather feedback

---

## The Solution

An all-in-one platform that provides:

### Core Features
1. **Patient Management** - Centralized digital records
2. **Smart Scheduling** - Online booking with automated reminders
3. **Workflow Automation** - Automated follow-ups and care journeys
4. **Campaign Management** - Email/SMS patient engagement
5 **Survey System** - Patient feedback and satisfaction tracking
6. **Staff Coordination** - Task management and team workflows
7. **Analytics Dashboard** - Real-time insights and reports
