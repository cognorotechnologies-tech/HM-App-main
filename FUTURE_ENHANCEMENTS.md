# 🚀 Future Enhancements for Hospital Management System

This document outlines potential functionalities that can be added to extend the HMS from Phase-1 OPD to a comprehensive healthcare platform.

## 🏥 **Patient Management Enhancements**

### 1. Patient Medical Records Repository
- Upload and store medical documents (lab reports, X-rays, CT scans)
- Document categorization and tagging
- Version history for updated documents

### 2. Patient Health Timeline/Journey
- Visual timeline of all medical interactions
- Health metrics tracking (BP, sugar levels, weight)
- Chronic disease progression tracking

### 3. Patient Portal Features
- Appointment cancellation/rescheduling
- Online payment for consultations
- Chat with doctor (async messaging)
- Upload symptoms/photos before appointment

### 4. Family/Dependent Management
- Link family members under one account
- Book appointments for dependents
- Shared medical history view (with consent)
